\pubentry{ACIIDS 2026 (CORE~B)}{
Thi Minh Chau Nguyen, Minh Hieu Nguyen, Duc Anh Nguyen, Xuan Huong Tran, Quoc Viet Hung Nguyen, Thanh Trung Huynh.\\
\textbf{LUMOS: LLM-Driven Contrastive Federated Sequential Recommendation}.\\
\emph{Contribution:} Participated in the development of a federated sequential recommendation framework where Large Language Models (LLMs) are used as on-device behavioural generators. Contributed to the design and experimentation of a tri-view contrastive learning mechanism that leverages predictive, paraphrased, and counterfactual sequences to address data sparsity and improve next-item prediction.
}

\pubentry{DAIS 2026 (CORE~C)}{

\textbf{Improving Federated Graph Recommendation with Semantic Guidance}.\\
\emph{Contribution:} Contributed to a semantic–structural federated graph recommendation framework where LLM-derived semantic embeddings guide the alignment of community-level structural representations across clients. Involved in experimental evaluation and visualization demonstrating improved robustness under non-IID data distributions.
